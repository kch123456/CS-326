About Echo Secure

A web-based platform that allows users to create, share, and manage notes in a secure manner. Uses client-side encryption to ensure notes are encrypted before they are stored on the server, making the data unreadable to anyone without the decryption key, including the service providers.

Goals and Objectives

  • Enhance security in note sharing and storage.
  • Implement robust client-side encryption.
  • Provide a user-friendly interface for managing encrypted notes.
